Actions for the Google Assistant.

Google's AI Assistant which was introduced back in 2016 with it's first successful phone named the Google Pixel is the Google Assistant. The Google Assistant which was a Pixel exclusive at the beginning, began to roll out to several devices and is now the most used Assistant. It is available on devices running android 5.0(Lollipop) and above. The Google Assistant is now on more than 400 million devices.

With the launch of the Google Assistanton several devices, Google introduced the Actions for the Google Assistant. The Actions for the Google Assistant was introduced to make the Assistant more productive and helpful. Since this feature was introduced, I have been creating several actions for the Google Assistant. Many Actions for the Google Assistant will be coming soon. Stay Tuned!!
