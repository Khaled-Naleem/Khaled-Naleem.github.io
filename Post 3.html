Voices of Horror

Alexa, the AI Assistant by Amazon has been reaching great heights in the past years. The Assistant had become people's choice due to the availability of the AI Assistant on the smart speakers by Amazon. Recently, Amazon introduced a new feature to the assistant named Alexa Skills.


There have been many skills under several categories made for the Amazon Alexa.


I have been working on a skill named "Voices of Horror". This is a skill which comes under the Games and Entertainment category including an immersive and a simulating choice-adventure, that would keep the users engaged with great interest.


The user plays the protagonist in this game. BUCKLE UP! to experience the spine chilling adventure you have never felt before!


Voices of Horror will be available on Amazon Alexa and all Alexa powered devices on the 10th of January 2019.
